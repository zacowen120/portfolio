Background

Kindred Table is the restaurant inside the Crowne Plaza Lansing West, an IHG hotel. The property needed to replace an underperforming restaurant with a fresh concept that appealed to locals and hotel guests alike.

Problem

In smaller markets, hotel restaurants are often seen as mass-produced and uninspiring. Nobody's first choice.

Process

Interviewed guests, locals, and industry leaders to learn what makes hotel restaurants succeed. Combined those learnings with market research, then led the development and launch of a brand new concept.

Insight

More than anything, their audience wants to feel like family when dining out. Hotel guests want a home away from home. And locals want a place to call their own. Together, they seek belonging, familiarity, and a bit of excitement.

Strategy

Let the feeling of family drive the entire concept. Kindred. A word for people who feel like family, even if they aren't related. Table. A gathering place where special moments happen. Together, they represent who the restaurant wants to be for its guests and its community.

Everyone just wanted to feel like family.
